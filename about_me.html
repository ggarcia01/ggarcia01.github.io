Bio:

Gilberto Garcia was born and raised in Chicago, Ill, but currently lives in Middletown, CT, where he is a student at Wesleyan University '20 studying Astronomy and Physics. At school, he is a co-founder of the Wesleyan Astronomy Club and the coordinator of Cross Street Tutoring. Gilberto is also volunteer for public outreach events, but especially enjoys those that involve operating Van Vleck Observatory's telescopes. Outside of academics, Gilberto plays club and intramural soccer and loves to attend Wesleyan's theater plays.

Back in Chicago, Gilberto has worked as a soccer referee at U.N.I.O.N Impact Center and at the Illinois Special Olympics. Whenever Gilberto has free time, he enjoys spending time with his siblings. He has two younger brothers, Anthony (Wesleyan '22) and Mateo, and a younger sister, Camila. He hopes to be a source of guidance and set a good example for them.

Gilberto is pursuing a career as an Astrophysicist.
