Hi, I'm Gilberto Garcia

welcome to my personal website

I currently live in Chicago, Ill, where I was born and raised. Previously, I lived in Middletown, CT for four years. During that time, I completed my undergraduate education at Wesleyan University, earning a degree in Astronomy and Physics. With these degrees, I am pursuing a career as an Astrophysicist. I am passionate about working towards minimizing the education access gap and providing resources to underrepresented students. Because of this, I have worked various tutoring positions in Chicago and Middletown. To learn more about my work and experience, you can take a look at my CV.

Outside of academics, I have enjoyed watching and playing soccer since I was a kid, playing for school and travel teams. I am also a general technology enthusiast; I like learning about anything ranging from programming languages to robotics and hardware.
